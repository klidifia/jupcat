What started with a tweet from @weremeow showing his "soulmate cat chilling in Acropolis" ignited the birth of $JUPCAT - the first memecoin in the Jupiter ecosystem. Hype quickly grew for $JUPCAT. But the developer abandoned shortly after launch, selling all tokens and locking Telegram. While disheartening; this did not defeat $JUPCAT! Passionate supporters swiftly regrouped and revived the community.

This dedicated group aims to build an even better version of $JUPCAT as the first community owned memecoin in the Jupiter ecosystem
